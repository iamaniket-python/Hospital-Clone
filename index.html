CT SCAN

A CT scan, or computed tomography scan,
is a medical imaging technique that uses
a combination of X-rays and computer technology to create detailed cross-sectional images of the body.

MRI

MRI machine, or magnetic resonance imaging
machine, is a medical imaging device that uses powerful
magnets, radio waves, and a computer to create
detailed images of the inside of the body.

X-RAY

An X-ray is a form of electromagnetic radiation
used to create images of the inside of the body. It’s one of the most common and widely used
imaging techniques in medicine for diagnosing and monitoring various conditions.
